Today we set up our desks at TrueStart after being accepted into their retail, consumer and fashion investment hub. We officially got the confirmation at exactly 12:35 on Thursday 19 June 2014 and our year-long accelerator search had ended! We have not only found an accelerator which truly understands our business as a B2B retail start-up but also has an incredible network full of leaders in our industry.

Since 2012 we have been working in two teams; with our sales team of Jude and my dad located in Slough and myself, Alessandro and more recently Usman working from my home bedroom in Edgware.

This doesn’t even compare with TrueStart’s workspace which will allow us to work together under one roof as a single team, connect with other exciting start-ups, mentors and most importantly – have access to TrueStart’s network of investors.

I can’t wait to start my first day tomorrow; regardless of it being a Monday morning or travelling into London during the rush-hour in summer. It’ll be a blast!
